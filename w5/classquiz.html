Quiz Day October 5

It may only be the fourth day of October, but with the air getting crisper and the weather getting cooler, snowy days are on their way.

To help prepare New England for the winter season, AccuWeather put out a 2021-2022 winter weather forecast.

Last year, Boston ended up with less snowfall than average, with a total of 38.6 inches compared to the usual average of 44.3 inches, according to AccuWeather. However, above-average snowfall amounts is expected this year, reaching as high as 55 inches
